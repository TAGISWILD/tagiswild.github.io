ABOUT

Atharva Chauhan, the owner and CEO of EthicCode Tech. Ltd., has played a pivotal role in the company's journey. Co-founder of the initial venture, Aysoat Ltd., Atharva underwent a significant transformation in December 2023, deciding to part ways with Aysoat due to conflicts and subsequently rebranding the company as EthicCode. Headquartered in Thane, Maharashtra, India, EthicCode is a leading software development company that prioritizes ethical practices in its operations.

Atharva's vision for EthicCode revolves around putting "Ethics First!" in software development. He is dedicated to delivering high-quality, user-friendly, and innovative software solutions while upholding a strong ethical code. With a commitment to customized business software solutions and comprehensive development services, EthicCode has successfully served a diverse clientele across various industries.

Atharva Chauhan's future endeavors for EthicCode include expanding the company's presence through new initiatives. This encompasses the launch of a tech podcast, EthicCodeCast, and engaging with a wider audience through platforms like Instagram, YouTube shorts, and TikTok (excluding India). Additionally, he plans to introduce Fun-Tech Project videos on the YouTube channel, showcasing versatility in the company's profile. Under Atharva's leadership, EthicCode remains dedicated to continued growth, promising innovation, and excellence in the field of ethical software development.
